Lightning Link Hold & Spin Mechanic Explained

Understanding the Hold & Spin feature is critical to maximizing your Lightning Link winning potential:

‚ö° Hold & Spin Complete Breakdown

Trigger Condition: Land 6 or more special symbols (pearls, coins, or themed symbols) anywhere on the 5 reels during a single spin. No scatter positions required - symbols can appear on any reel in any position.

Initial Setup: When triggered, all special symbols lock in place showing credit values ranging from 1x to 50x your total bet. You receive 3 re-spins with only special symbols and blank positions appearing on reels.

Re-Spin Mechanics: Each re-spin can land new special symbols or blanks. Any new special symbol locks in place and resets your re-spin counter back to 3. This continues until either: (A) You run out of re-spins with no new symbols landing, or (B) You fill all 15 positions with special symbols (triggers Grand Jackpot automatically).

Jackpot Thresholds:

  • Mini Jackpot: Land 3 special symbols total - Pays 10-20x total bet
  • Minor Jackpot: Land 6 special symbols total - Pays 30-50x total bet
  • Major Jackpot: Land 12 special symbols total - Pays 100-300x total bet
  • Grand Jackpot: Fill all 15 positions - Pays 1,000-1,088x total bet ($50,000+ at max bets)

Credit Values: Each locked symbol displays a credit value. Your total win equals the sum of all credit values PLUS any jackpots triggered. A full screen (Grand Jackpot) with high credit values can exceed 2,000x total bet!
