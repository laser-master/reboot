Troubleshooting Login Issues

Common Login Problems & Solutions

Forgot Password

Click on "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email or mobile number, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password. The reset link is valid for 24 hours.

Account Locked

If you've entered wrong passwords multiple times, your account may be temporarily locked for security. Wait for 30 minutes or contact our customer support team for immediate assistance.

Invalid Credentials

Double-check your username/email and password. Ensure caps lock is off. Make sure you're using credentials from the correct Good Slots account. If you have multiple accounts, verify you're using the right one.

Browser or Technical Issues

Clear your browser cache and cookies, or try using a different browser. Ensure JavaScript is enabled. Try restarting your device. Check if you have the latest browser version installed.

Network or Connection Problems

Check your internet connection. Try switching between WiFi and mobile data. Ensure you have sufficient network coverage. Try login again after waiting a few minutes.

OTP Not Received

Wait up to 2 minutes for the OTP SMS. Check your spam or junk folder if using email verification. Request a new OTP if you don't receive one. Verify your phone number is correct in your profile.

Login Security Tips for Indian Players

  • Use Strong Passwords: Create a unique password with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of security by enabling 2FA in your account settings.
  • Avoid Public WiFi: Don't login to your Good Slots account on public or unsecured WiFi networks.
  • Regular Password Updates: Change your password every 3-6 months for enhanced security.
  • Monitor Account Activity: Regularly check your login history and report any suspicious activity immediately.
  • Keep Contact Info Updated: Ensure your email and phone number are always current for account recovery.
  • Use Official Apps Only: Download Good Slots apps only from our official website or authorized app stores to avoid phishing.
  • Never Share Credentials: Never give your password or OTP to anyone, even customer support staff.
  • Logout After Gaming: Always logout from your account, especially on shared devices.
