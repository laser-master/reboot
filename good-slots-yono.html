How do I connect my Yono SBI account to Good Slots? +
Go to your Good Slots payment settings, select "Connect Yono Account", and follow the authentication process. You'll be redirected to Yono SBI login, and after authorization, your accounts will be linked. The entire process takes less than 2 minutes.

Yes, it's completely safe. Good Slots uses bank-level encryption and never stores your banking credentials. All authentication is handled directly by SBI's secure servers. Your Yono account credentials are never shared with Good Slots.
What are the transaction fees for Yono deposits? +
Yono deposits through Good Slots have minimal fees as per SBI's standard transaction charges. For most deposits, there are no additional fees on top of regular SBI transfer charges. Check your Yono account statements for exact fees.
