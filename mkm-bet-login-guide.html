Login Troubleshooting

"Invalid Email or Password" Error

Make sure you've entered your email and password correctly. Check for capital letters and spaces. If you're sure your details are correct, try the password reset process.

Account Locked

If you enter your password incorrectly multiple times, your account may be temporarily locked for security. Wait 30 minutes and try again, or use the password reset feature.

Can't Receive Reset Email

Check your spam/junk folder. Add notifications@mkmbetindia.com to your contacts. Try logging in with a different email address if you have multiple registered accounts.

App Won't Login

Update the MKM Bet app to the latest version. Clear the app's cache in settings. Uninstall and reinstall the app. Try logging in using the web browser instead.

Cookie & Cache Issues

Clear your browser cache and cookies. Disable browser extensions temporarily. Try using Incognito/Private browsing mode. Try a different browser.
