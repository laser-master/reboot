How to Play Teen Patti 365

1

Basic Setup

Teen Patti 365 is played with 3-6 players using a standard 52-card deck. Each player receives 3 cards, and the goal is to have the best three-card hand.

2

Hand Rankings

Hands are ranked from highest to lowest: Trail (three of a kind), Pure Sequence, Sequence, Color, Pair, and High Card. The best hand wins the pot.

3

Betting Options

Players can bet blind (without seeing cards) or seen (after seeing cards). Blind betting costs half but seen players can bet double the amount.

4

Winning the Game

The player with the best hand wins the pot. In case of ties, the pot is split. Show your cards if confident, or pack if you think you have a weak hand.
