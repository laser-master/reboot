How to Play Rummy 365

1

Understand the Basics

Rummy 365 is played with 2-6 players using a standard 52-card deck. Each player is dealt 13 cards, and the objective is to arrange these cards into valid sets and sequences.

2

Form Valid Combinations

Create at least two sequences (one must be pure) and arrange remaining cards in sets or sequences. A pure sequence doesn't use jokers, while an impure sequence can use jokers.

3

Declare Your Hand

Once you've arranged all 13 cards into valid combinations, declare your hand. If your declaration is valid, you win the game and your opponents' points are calculated.

4

Score Calculation

Face cards (J, Q, K, A) carry 10 points each. Number cards carry their face value. The goal is to have the minimum points in your hand when someone declares.
