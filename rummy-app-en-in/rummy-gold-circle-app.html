Game Variants Available

Points Rummy

The fastest format where players compete for points. Win instantly when you form valid sequences and sets. Points are converted to cash based on the stake.

Pool Rummy

Players compete in pools (e.g., 101 pool or 201 pool). Players are eliminated based on accumulated points. Last remaining player wins the pool prize.

Deals Rummy

Fixed number of deals where players compete for a predetermined number of rounds. Each round is scored, and final winner is determined by total points.

Tournament Rummy

Competitive tournaments with multiple rounds and elimination stages. Players compete for significant prize pools and rankings.
